# Planning Job Config

## Intent
- Use `planning/` for roadmap reconciliation, milestone decomposition, candidate job sequencing, release capability planning, and non-executable scoping work.

## Defaults
- Planning jobs are non-runtime by default.
- Planning outputs are advisory until converted into complete executable job templates or explicit active-run instructions.
- Safe job sizing should bias toward small, reviewable, whitelist-bound executable jobs.

## Recommended Fields
- capability or milestone target
- dependencies
- candidate job sequence
- risk level
- likely whitelist sketch
- verification strategy
- non-goals
